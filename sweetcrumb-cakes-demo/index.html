FAQ

Questions before you order?

How much advance notice is needed?

One to two weeks is recommended for most custom cakes, with more notice for weddings and larger dessert tables.

Do you offer eggless cakes?

Yes, eggless options are available for select flavours. Mention this in your quote request.

Do you deliver?

Pickup is standard in Brampton. Local delivery may be available depending on date, distance, order size, and setup needs.

Is a deposit required?

Yes. A deposit is required once your quote is approved so your event date can be reserved. The remaining balance is due before pickup or delivery.

Can I send inspiration photos?

Yes. Inspiration photos help with style direction, but final designs are customized for each order.

How many servings should I order?

Tell us your guest count and whether cake is the main dessert or part of a larger dessert table. We’ll recommend a size that fits your event.

Do you handle allergies?

Please mention allergies or dietary requests in your quote form. Some options may be available, but orders are prepared in a kitchen that may handle common allergens.

Can you take rush orders?

Rush orders may be possible depending on the date, design, and order size. Sending your request early gives the best chance of availability.
