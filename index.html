FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

How much does a website cost?

Launch starts at $1,500 CAD. Growth (most popular for trades) is $3,000. Custom projects start from $4,500 for heavier strategy, integrations, and more complex lead capture. 50/50 payment split — half to start, half on launch day. See full pricing →

How long does it take?

2–4 weeks from deposit to live site. The timeline depends mostly on how quickly you can send content (photos, services list, contact info). I handle the rest.

Do I need to have everything ready before we start?

No. A few phone photos, your services list, and your contact info is enough to get going. No logo? No problem — I can work without one or suggest simple options. I’ll walk you through exactly what I need in the kick-off call.

Will I be able to update the site myself?

Yes. After launch I do a 15-minute walkthrough showing you how to update text, photos, hours, and services yourself. You won’t need to call me for small changes.

Is hosting included?

Hosting setup is included. I’ll prepare the site for Cloudflare Pages (free tier) and walk you through connecting your domain. Most clients pay $0/month to host after launch.

What if I’m not happy with the result?

Full refund within the first 30 days if the work doesn’t meet the agreed scope. No questions asked. I’d rather fix it than fight about it.
