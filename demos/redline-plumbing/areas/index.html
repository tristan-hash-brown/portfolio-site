Local service coverage

Plumbing service across Hamilton and nearby cities

Our trucks run out of Hamilton. We typically reach customers across the service area within 30–60 minutes.

Where Redline works

Every city below gets the full range of services — emergency calls, drains, water heaters, leaks, fixtures, and sump pumps.

Hamilton

⚡ 30–45 min response

Our home base. We cover all Hamilton neighbourhoods — the downtown core, the mountain, the east end, Waterdown, Binbrook, and everywhere in between. Same-day service most days.

Stoney Creek

⚡ 30–50 min response

Rapid residential growth means a wide mix of housing ages. We handle both the older bungalows in the lower city and newer builds on the escarpment — fast response for drain and pipe emergencies.

Ancaster

⚡ 35–55 min response

Established neighbourhoods with many homes that benefit from timely plumbing maintenance and upgrades. We regularly service homes throughout the Meadowlands area and along Wilson Street.

Dundas

⚡ 35–55 min response

Heritage homes and valley clay soil make root intrusion in drain lines common here. We know the area's quirks and carry the right equipment for the job.

Burlington

⚡ 40–60 min response

We service Aldershot, Brant Hills, Millcroft, and the downtown core. Burlington is at the edge of our regular area — call to confirm availability for your location.
