Find It & Fix It

Leak Repair

Even a small leak wastes thousands of litres per year and can silently rot walls, floors, and framing. We locate leaks — including hidden ones behind walls and under slabs — and fix them permanently.

  • Dripping faucets and fixture leaks
  • Pipe leaks behind walls or ceilings
  • Visible and hidden leak investigation
  • Pipe and valve repair or replacement
  • Post-repair pressure testing
