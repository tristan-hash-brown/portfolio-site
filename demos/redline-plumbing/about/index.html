15+ years of practical plumbing service

Redline was founded by a licensed master plumber who grew up in Hamilton's east end and trained in the trades straight out of high school. One truck. One simple philosophy: show up on time, do the job right, charge a fair price.

Fifteen years later, still family-owned. Every plumber who comes to your door is a licensed, insured member of our team — no subcontractors we can't vouch for. We work primarily with homeowners across Hamilton, Stoney Creek, Ancaster, Dundas, and Burlington. We know the housing stock, the older pipe materials, and the seasonal issues that come with this climate.
