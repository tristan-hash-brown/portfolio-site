Local service coverage

Electrical service across the GTA west.

Our vans run out of Brampton. We reach most customers across our service area within 30–60 minutes for urgent calls.

Where SparkRight works

Every city below gets the full range of services — troubleshooting, panel upgrades, EV chargers, lighting, outlets, and renovation wiring.

Brampton

⚡ 25–40 min response

Our home base. We cover all Brampton neighbourhoods — downtown, Bramalea, Heart Lake, Springdale, and everywhere in between. Same-day service most days for urgent calls.

Mississauga

⚡ 30–50 min response

One of our most active service areas. We cover Streetsville, Port Credit, Erin Mills, Meadowvale, and the city centre — older homes and new builds alike.

Caledon

⚡ 40–60 min response

Rural and estate homes in Caledon often have unique electrical demands — older wiring, generator hookups, and detached shop wiring. We know the area well.

Etobicoke

⚡ 35–55 min response

Established neighbourhoods with a mix of older bungalows and newer builds. We regularly service homes in Rexdale, Humber Valley, and along the lakeshore.

Oakville

⚡ 45–65 min response

We service Kerr Village, Glen Abbey, and River Oaks. Oakville is at the edge of our regular area — call to confirm availability for your address.
