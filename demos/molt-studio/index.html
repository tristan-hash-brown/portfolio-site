About the maker

A small studio built around texture, contrast, and presence.

MOLT Studio is a fictional jewelry practice founded by Mara Vale, a Toronto-based jewelry maker working from a small bench studio. Pieces move through pop-ups, Instagram drops, and custom requests.

The work is shaped around sterling silver, bronze accents, oxidized finishes, and pieces that feel unearthed rather than polished flat: substantial, imperfect, and personal enough to keep close. Custom work typically takes 4-6 weeks from design notes to finished piece.

The site turns scattered interest from DMs into a clearer path: browse the drop, understand the custom process, and join the next release before it disappears.

Before you ask

Useful details for drop days and custom work.

How long does custom work take?

Most custom rings, pendants, and gift commissions take 4-6 weeks after the design direction and size are confirmed.

Can I request ring sizing help?

Yes. Mara can guide you through simple at-home sizing notes before a custom ring is started.

Are pieces one-of-one?

Drop pieces are made in tiny batches, while custom commissions are one-off pieces shaped around your request.

What materials and finishes are used?

The fictional studio works with sterling silver, bronze details, oxidized surfaces, hammered texture, and hand-polished highlights.

Can pieces be ordered as gifts?

Yes. Gift commissions can include sizing guidance, a finish recommendation, and simple wrapped packaging.
