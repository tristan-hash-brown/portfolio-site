FAQ

Common preorder questions.

How far ahead should I preorder?

Weekend snack boxes are usually requested several days ahead. Larger party trays are easier to quote with at least one to two weeks of notice.

Do you offer mild options?

Yes, mild and mixed spice preferences can be requested. Final options depend on the weekly menu and order size.

Can I order party trays?

Yes. Party trays can be requested for birthdays, office lunches, family gatherings, and community events, then quoted by size.

Is delivery available?

Brampton pickup is the standard option. Delivery may be available for larger event orders by request, depending on timing and location.

Do you handle allergies?

Please include allergy notes before ordering. We’ll confirm ingredients and preparation details before accepting the order.

Do you require a deposit?

A deposit may be requested after the order is quoted, especially for party trays or larger catering inquiries.

Are these prices final?

Menu prices may vary by quantity, substitutions, and event size. Party trays are quoted after we review your guest count and item choices.

What happens after I submit the form?

We’ll review your request and reply with availability, pricing, pickup timing, and any deposit details before the order is confirmed.

Planning a weekend order or party tray?

Send the date, guest count, items, and pickup request so the order can be quoted clearly.
