Process

From first message to live site in 2-4 weeks.

We handle the tech. You focus on your jobs. Here's exactly what happens and when you're needed.

The timeline

What happens each week.

  1. Wk 1Kick-off

    30-min kick-off call — you send photos, info, and colours

    We confirm your services, service area, goals, and tone. You send over photos, logo (if you have one), and 200 words about your business. That's it — I take it from there.

    📋  Your input: photos, logo, 200 words about the business
  2. Wk 2–3Design

    Design mockups — you pick your favourite, we refine

    I build two design options based on your content and trade. You pick the direction you prefer, give quick feedback, and I refine. No design experience needed — just "yes, that" or "more like this."

    👀  Your input: feedback on 2 options (1–2 hours total)
  3. Wk 4Build

    Live build and testing — you check it on your phone, we fix

    The full site goes live on a staging link. You open it on your phone, tap through it like a customer would, and flag anything that feels off. I fix it — usually same day.

    📱  Your input: 30-min mobile review on your own phone
  4. Wk 5Launch

    Go live + 15-min training walkthrough

    We connect your domain, run final checks on all links, forms, and mobile layout, then flip the switch. I walk you through how to update text and photos yourself in a 15-minute call — so you're never stuck waiting on me for small edits.

    🚀  Your input: domain access + 15-min walkthrough call

What I need from you

Less than 3 hours of your time, total.

You don't need a perfect brief or any design knowledge. These are the only things I need to build a site that works for your business.

  • Business name, phone, email
  • Services you offer
  • Cities or areas you cover
  • A few job photos (phone photos are fine)
  • Reviews or proof points if you have them
  • Logo (optional — I can work without one)
  • Link to your current site (if any)
