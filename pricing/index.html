FAQ

Straight answers to common questions.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. 50/50 split — half upfront to start, half on launch day. No hidden fees.

How long does it take?

2–4 weeks from deposit to live, depending on how quickly you provide content, photos, business details, account access, and feedback.

Can I update the site myself?

Most clients send small updates to me. Self-editing is available only if we set the site up on a CMS or editor-friendly platform.

What if I don't like it?

If we are not aligned after the initial direction stage, you can cancel before production begins. Once production work is approved and underway, refunds are limited to unstarted work and the agreed project scope.

Is hosting included?

Hosting setup guidance is included. I'll prepare the site for Cloudflare Pages or a similar simple static host and walk you through connecting your domain.

Can I start small and add later?

Yes. Start with Launch and upgrade as you grow. The code is clean and easy to expand.
