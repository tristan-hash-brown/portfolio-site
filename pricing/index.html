Pricing FAQ

Common questions before starting.

Do I need to have all my content ready?

No. It helps to have services, service areas, contact details, and any photos or reviews, but the project can start with a simple content checklist.

Can you help with copy?

Yes. I can help shape practical website copy from your service list, service areas, common jobs, and proof points. Larger copywriting needs may affect scope.

Is hosting included?

Hosting setup depends on the project. I can help prepare the static site for a simple host such as Cloudflare Pages and note what needs to be connected.

Can I start small and add pages later?

Yes. A focused first version can launch with the most important services and pages, then expand as the business has more content or proof.

What happens after launch?

You receive the finished static website files, launch notes, and any agreed handoff details. Monthly care is optional for small updates and checks.
