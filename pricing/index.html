FAQ

Straight answers to common questions.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. 50/50 split — half upfront to start, half on launch day. No hidden fees.

How long does it take?

2–4 weeks from deposit to live, depending on how quickly you can share content (photos, services, contact info).

Can I update the site myself?

Yes. I'll show you how to make basic edits (text, photos, hours) in a 15-minute walkthrough after launch.

What if I don't like it?

Full refund in the first 30 days if the work doesn't meet the agreed scope. No questions asked.

Is hosting included?

Hosting setup guidance is included. I'll prepare the site for Cloudflare Pages or a similar simple static host and walk you through connecting your domain.

Can I start small and add later?

Yes. Start with Starter and upgrade as you grow. The code is clean and easy to expand.
