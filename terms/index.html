These are the plain-English terms for working with Tristan Web Studio. They cover the Website Review and website projects.

Who provides the services

Services are provided by Tristan Web Studio.

The Website Review

The Website Review is an advisory review of your existing website. It is feedback and recommendations — it is not a full website rebuild and does not include making changes to your site.

Website projects

Website projects require scope confirmation before work begins. We agree on what is included before any production work starts.

Quotes and timelines

Quotes and timelines depend on project details, such as the pages involved, the content provided, and the complexity of the work.

Your responsibilities

You are responsible for providing accurate information, content, approvals, and any access needed to complete the work on time.

Results

Tristan Web Studio does not guarantee specific traffic, search rankings, leads, or revenue. The goal is a clear, trustworthy website that makes it easy for visitors to contact you.

Payment

Payment terms are shown at checkout or set out in written project communication.
