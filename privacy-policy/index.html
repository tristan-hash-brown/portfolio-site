Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This policy explains what information Tristan Web Studio collects, why it is collected, and the tools used to handle it. It is written in plain language.

Information that may be collected

Depending on how you contact me or what you buy, I may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business name
  • Website URL
  • Project details you share
  • Form submissions
  • Payment-related information through Stripe

Payment card details are handled by Stripe at checkout. I do not see or store your full card number.

Why it is collected

This information is used to:

  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Deliver Website Reviews
  • Prepare quotes
  • Communicate about projects
  • Process payments

Third-party tools

I use a small number of trusted services to run the studio. When you use them, your information is handled by those services under their own privacy terms:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Tally — project inquiry and intake forms
  • Formspree — the general contact form
  • Calendly — booking calls

Selling your information

Your information is not sold.

Access, correction, or deletion

You can ask to access, correct, or delete the information I hold about you. Email hello@tristanwebstudio.com and I will take care of it.

Questions about this policy? Email hello@tristanwebstudio.com. See also our Terms and Refund Policy.
